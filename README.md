## 🟡 What Are the Five Senses?

The five senses are special ways our body collects information from the environment.

| Sense | Body Part | Example |
|------|------|------|
| Sight | Eyes | Seeing a rainbow |
| Hearing | Ears | Listening to music |
| Smell | Nose | Smelling cookies |
| Taste | Tongue | Tasting ice cream |
| Touch | Skin | Feeling something soft |

### Simple Examples

- Eyes help us see colors.
- Ears help us hear sounds.
- Nose helps us smell scents.
- Tongue helps us taste food.
- Skin helps us feel textures.

## 🧠 How Each Sense Works

### 👀 Sight
Eyes capture light and send messages to the brain.

### 👂 Hearing
Ears detect sound waves and help us hear.

### 👃 Smell
The nose senses tiny particles in the air.

### 👅 Taste
Taste buds recognize sweet, sour, salty, bitter, and umami flavors.

### ✋ Touch
Skin senses pressure, temperature, and texture.
